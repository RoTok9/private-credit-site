Blue Owl Credit Income Corp (OCIC)

A forensic audit of the $26.4B non-traded BDC managed by Blue Owl Credit Advisers. Twelve red flags identified from public SEC filings: Rating 3 (stressed) loans surged 172% in nine months to $1.15B; four consecutive quarters of unrealized losses in 2024; distribution coverage at exactly 1.00x with zero buffer; and a valuation gap of approximately $2.35 per share between OCIC's stated NAV ($9.57) and the market-implied liquidation value (~$7.22) derived from OBDC — the publicly traded vehicle run by the same manager investing in the same loans.

Vol. 6 — Forensic Audit: Blue Owl Credit Income Corp

The Buried Deterioration

Audit Thesis

OCIC's headline NAV has been managed to near-flatness — $9.53 to $9.57 across all of 2024. But the internal portfolio rating data tells a different story. Rating 3 (Below Expectations) loans grew 172% in nine months. Rating 5 (Anticipated Loss) jumped from $3M to $31.5M. Every single quarter of 2024 produced realized and unrealized investment losses. And the publicly traded vehicle run by the same manager — OBDC — trades at a 24.5% discount to its own stated NAV. Applied to OCIC, that discount implies a market-clearing value of approximately $7.22 per share — a $2.35 gap against the stated $9.57 that retail investors are buying at quarterly.
