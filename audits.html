Fund Audits

Bottom-up forensic audits of private credit vehicles — non-traded BDCs, interval funds, and similar structures. Each audit is built entirely from public SEC filings, applying a consistent analytical framework: internal rating migration, distribution coverage integrity, NAV stress-testing, market-implied valuation proxies, and a scored red flag inventory.
